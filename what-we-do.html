At Advent AI, we’re more than consultants — we’re builders, trainers, and partners in your AI journey. Each of our services is designed to help you adopt AI confidently, with solutions that are as practical as they are innovative.

Application Engineering

Think of us as your co-architects in building applications with an AI flavor, that don't just look good on paper but actually deliver results in the real world.

We begin by understanding your business challenges. Is it translating your ideas into an application? Is it understanding the user requirements? Is it building specific features that seem difficult to implement with the available resources? Is it manual processes eating up time? Customer interactions needing personalization? Or compliance checks slowing you down? From there, we design applications that solves these problems step by step.

Our phased approach covers everything:

  • Discovery & Design: We listen to your teams, study workflows, and design applications that match the ideas in your mind.
  • Full-Cycle Development: From preparing data to developing custom applications, we handle the heavy lifting.
  • Iterative Prototyping: No big-bang surprises—we build and test in small steps, giving you a chance to see progress and course-correct early.
  • Deployment & Support: Once live, we stay by your side with security, monitoring, user training, and continuous application improvements.

The impact: Applications that keep you at the centre of the design, development and deployment process.

AI Consulting

AI can feel like a maze — so many tools, so many promises. That’s where we step in as your guides.

Our consulting is not about delivering thick reports — it’s about giving you clarity and confidence. We start by assessing your readiness: Do you have the right data? Is your infrastructure AI‑friendly? Are your teams prepared for change?

From there, we:

  • Map Out Opportunities: Identify where AI can drive real ROI — whether in supply chain, pharma, manufacturing, or services.
  • Design Roadmaps: A clear path with milestones, budgets, and change management steps.
  • Select the Right Tools: From generative AI to predictive models, we recommend what fits your context.
  • Pilot Projects: Build small-scale proofs‑of‑concept to show value and reduce risk.
  • Enable Change: Equip your teams with the knowledge and mindset to embrace AI with confidence.

The result: A future-ready business that doesn't just adopt AI — it thrives with it.

Roadmap Audits

Sometimes organizations are already on their AI journey — but aren't sure if they're on the right track. That's where our Roadmap Audits come in.

Think of it as an AI health check. We examine every aspect of your strategy and execution:

  • Alignment: Is your AI strategy truly connected to business objectives?
  • Technology Fit: Are you using the right models and tools, or overspending on unnecessary features?
  • Costs: Are licensing and cloud expenses optimized, or are there hidden fees eating into ROI?
  • Performance: Are your systems accurate, resilient, and fast enough for real‑world demands?
  • Governance: Are compliance, data privacy, and risk controls properly in place?

Outcome: Confidence that your AI journey is cost‑efficient, compliant, and scalable for growth.

Customised Training

Technology only succeeds when people embrace it. That’s why training is at the heart of what we do.

Instead of one‑size‑fits‑all workshops, we design personalized programs tailored to different stakeholders:

  • Executives & Leaders: Understand AI strategy, governance, and risks so you can lead with vision.
  • Technical Teams: Dive deep into model development, pipelines, and troubleshooting.
  • Functional Teams: Learn how AI can streamline daily tasks like analytics, reporting, or compliance.
  • End Users: Build hands‑on confidence in using AI systems through interactive, scenario‑based modules.

We also use AI‑powered platforms to deliver adaptive learning paths — so people get exactly the training they need, at their own pace.

Why it matters:

  • Teams adopt AI faster and with more confidence.
  • Skills stay future‑proof as technology evolves.
  • Organizations see quicker ROI because people actually use the tools effectively.

In short: whether you’re just exploring AI, already building, or scaling up — we’re here to guide, build, audit, and train every step of the way.
