Hospital Equipment Planning Intelligence Agent

AI-Driven Multi-Department CapEx Planning for Healthcare

Overview

Advent AI designs and builds an internal AI-driven planning agent for one of the largest healthcare consultancies in India. The agent lets users select hospital departments, set bed numbers and budget limits, choose equipment classification levels, and automatically generate full equipment lists with costs, procurement timelines, and CapEx summaries, eliminating the time-consuming, manual effort.

The Problem

  • Excel-Based Master Lists — Equipment data scattered across multiple spreadsheets with no unified source of truth.
  • Manual Planning Burden — Time-consuming, error-prone manual effort to compile equipment lists for each department.
  • No Bed Capacity Scaling — Equipment quantities not automatically adjusted for different hospital bed capacities.
  • No Budget Constraint Support — Difficult to meet budget limits while balancing equipment quality and availability.
  • Inconsistent Pricing and No Optimization — Multi-source pricing data with no intelligent optimization or lead-time risk awareness.

What We Built

An AI intelligence agent that automates the entire hospital equipment planning workflow:

  • Multi-Department Selector — Users select departments (e.g., ICU, Surgery, Radiology, etc.).
  • Bed-Number Input with Auto-Scaling — Set bed capacity per department; agent auto-calculates equipment quantities based on bed ratios.
  • Budget Allocation Per Department — Define budget limits for each department.
  • Three-Tier Equipment Costing — Standard, Mid-tier, and Premium options for cost-sensitive planning.
  • Procurement Lead Time Integration — Equipment categorized by lead times (immediate, near-term, future).
  • Automated CapEx Summary Dashboard — Real-time CapEx tracking, department-wise cost breakdown, and procurement timeline visibility.

Key Capabilities

A. Smart Equipment Generation

  • ✓ Bed-Ratio-Based Auto Quantity Calculation — Equipment quantities automatically scaled based on bed count and departmental ratios.
  • ✓ Classification Filtering — Filter equipment by Primary, Secondary, or Tertiary classification levels.

B. Multi-Tier Costing Engine

  • ✓ Three Pricing Tiers — Standard, Mid-tier, and Premium cost options per equipment.
  • ✓ Auto-Recommends Tier Based on Budget and Lead-Time Risk — Intelligent tier selection to meet budget and procurement constraints.
  • ✓ Real-Time CapEx Recalculation — Instant updates when users adjust department selections, bed counts, or budget limits.

C. Procurement Intelligence

  • ✓ Lead-Time Categories — Equipment grouped by lead times: Under 30 days, 30 to 90 days, Over 90 days.
  • ✓ Procurement Sequence — Automatic ordering: Immediate (stock on hand), Near-term (30–90 days), Future (90+ days).

D. Summary Dashboard

  • ✓ Department-Wise CapEx vs Budget — Visual comparison of planned CapEx against allocated budget per department.
  • ✓ Cost-Tier Breakdown — Clear visibility of Standard, Mid-tier, and Premium cost distribution.
  • ✓ Procurement Timeline View — Roadmap showing immediate, near-term, and future procurement phases.
  • ✓ Automation Coverage Tracking — Percentage of planning automated, manual intervention flags.

Outcomes Targeted

  • Accurate bed-scaled equipment quantities across all departments.
  • Correct classification filtering (Primary, Secondary, Tertiary).
  • Accurate CapEx totals including GST and other taxes.
  • Procurement timelines visible and optimized for lead-time risk.
  • Budget-constrained planning with smart tier recommendations.
  • Manual effort reduced by more than 80%.
  • Automation coverage exceeding 80% (below 20% manual intervention).

Conclusion

Advent AI turns a complex, spreadsheet-driven planning process into an intelligent, automated agent, giving one of the largest healthcare consultancies in India a faster, more accurate, and data-driven equipment planning workflow.
